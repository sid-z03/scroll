ABOUT

Azeem Mohammad is a cinematographer based in Hyderabad, India. His work explores intimacy, memory, and time — often through natural light and grounded realism.

Each frame is guided by a commitment to visual honesty, translating story into imagery that breathes. He asks how light itself can carry emotion — favouring restraint over spectacle, and texture over polish.

Working between India and the UK.
